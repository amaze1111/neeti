Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026

Neeti provides a multiplayer board game experience. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as display name, email address, Firebase account identifiers, and connected login provider.
  • Gameplay information such as match participation, scores, readiness state, etc.
  • Technical and analytics information such as app events, device/app usage details, and backend event records used to improve reliability and measure user journeys.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users and maintain app sessions.
  • To run multiplayer matches, resolve gameplay state, and prevent abuse.
  • To analyze product usage and improve gameplay, onboarding, matchmaking, and retention.

Authentication Providers

Neeti may use Firebase Authentication and providers such as Email/Password, Google, and Facebook. Those services may process personal data according to their own privacy terms.

Analytics

Neeti may use Firebase Analytics and backend event storage to understand player journeys, game completion, feature usage, and operational integrity.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may use service providers necessary to operate the game, such as hosting, authentication, analytics, and database infrastructure.

Data Retention

We retain account and gameplay data for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain game integrity, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and support deletion requests.

Your Choices

  • You may stop using the service at any time.
  • You may request deletion of your account data using the process on the user data deletion page.
